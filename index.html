What is ZERO?

ZERO is a symbolic crypto project. It exists to remind participants of a simple truth: crypto is impermanent.

ZERO does not promise profit. ZERO does not guarantee growth. ZERO is not an investment.

Why ZERO exists

As crypto becomes legalized, more people will enter the market. Many will do so without understanding risk.

ZERO was created by someone who lost everything — to remind others before they do.
