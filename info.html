What is ZERO?

ZERO is a symbolic crypto project. It exists to remind participants of one simple truth: crypto can go to zero anytime.

ZERO does not promise profit. ZERO does not sell dreams. ZERO does not guarantee outcomes.

Why does ZERO exist?

Most losses in crypto do not come from lack of knowledge, but from ignoring risk.

ZERO was created after loss, not before success. It exists as a pause — a moment to think before acting.

What ZERO is NOT

ZERO is not an investment product.
ZERO is not financial advice.
ZERO is not a get-rich-quick scheme.

Responsibility

Participation in ZERO is voluntary. Anyone interacting with ZERO accepts that total loss is possible.
